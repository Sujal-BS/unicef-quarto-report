Vitamin A Supplementation: Saving Children's Lives Worldwide

Sujal Boodgere Sunil - A00096721 | BAA1030 UNICEF Data Analyst Report

Executive Summary

Vitamin A is essential for good vision, strong immunity, and healthy growth in young children. Lack of Vitamin A (deficiency) is a serious but preventable problem, especially in developing countries. It can cause blindness and makes children more likely to die from common illnesses like measles and diarrhoea.
